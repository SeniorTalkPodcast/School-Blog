The Students' Point of View on the Advantages of Online Classes

In my opinion online classes are a great way to keep learning and having a schedule that accommodates to your needs. I also think it is great to be be independent and responsable.

By: Victor Santos

Online classes offer a number of significant advantages. First, they foster increased accountability by requiring greater self-discipline and organisation to meet deadlines and assignments. In addition, they provide unlimited access to online information and resources, allowing us to delve deeper into specific topics and clarify doubts quickly and efficiently. The convenience of being at home is also remarkable, as it eliminates the need to travel, which in turn saves time and reduces stress related to traffic.

By: Yael Donis

Online classes offer greater flexibility in terms of scheduling, allowing students to get more rest or other activities instead of having to travel to and from school. In addition, by being able to take classes from anywhere with an internet connection, students have the opportunity to participate in pre-activities or make commitments without missing out on educational content. The availability of online resources also eliminates the need to carry physical books, making it easier to study anywhere. In addition, the ability to take notes online may be more efficient for some students, allowing them to review material more quickly and ask pertinent questions during class.

By: Valeria Cifuentes

Students can adjust lessons to fit their daily schedules thanks to flexible scheduling, which is useful for those who have obligations to their families or jobs. The ability to view class recordings and have access to digital resources can also help with learning.
